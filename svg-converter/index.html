SVG to PNG converter

When generating SVG elements with libraries such as D3.js or Rune.js, you often need the ability to convert your SVG to a pixel-based image format. However, tools like Illustrator or Inkscape are incredibly buggy, and do not render SVG's like the browser. This tool lets you choose an SVG file to convert, choose the size of the output image, and render the final PNG via the browser.
